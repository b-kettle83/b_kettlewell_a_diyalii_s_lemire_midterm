Comedy vs Action vs Drama: A Truly Cinematic Battle

Why Comedy Is The Greatest

When you’re watching a movie, you’re there to relax and have fun for one reason or another. Some people get their enjoyment from being scared, or from intense sequences, but everybody can find enjoyment in laughter and just having a good time. Action and drama are very intense genres of movies, which while they may have a time and place, they aren’t as usable options or as widely available to the viewer as a comedy movie might be, whereas with comedy, everyone can always use a laugh. Also, comedy is something nearly everybody will enjoy with friends or family, while something like action or drama asks for a more particular taste that not everybody will have. Comedy can furthermore help lift spirits and weaken the bad things going on by giving an opportunity to just be in the moment and laugh, and as the saying goes, laughter is the best medicine.

Why Action is #1

When someone goes to the movies they end up seeing a larger ratio of action movies compared to any other. That is because movie directors understand that their target audience eats it up. Many action movies can be for any target group, such as kids, adults, or elderly. By example: Spider-man is a favourite to many children around the world. Then there’s even movies for a man’s wife that might’ve preferred to watch a rom-com, such as Fallout. Action involves some teamwork with other genres such as comedy, drama, and even romance. Action movies are the pinnacle of anticipation, excitement and adrenaline. When watching in 3D it even feels like you’re experiencing the movie as if you are a character in it. It becomes so enticing that you get caught up in the movie and the whole experience. Although Comedy and Drama can be great genres to watch, they don't give off as much of an adrenaline rush of happiness while watching. Comedy is great for a laugh and drama makes you think about your own self. But to escape from that, Action is the way to go.

Drama

Why It Is Best

Drama is the best genre because it dives deep into human emotions and tells relatable, meaningful stories. Unlike other genres, drama makes viewers reflect on their own lives, creating a stronger bond to the characters and plot. With powerful storytelling, dramas blend feelings like love, loss, and hope, offering a meaningful experience. Drama changes the way we view life. One drama that inspired me is Into The Wild. It tells the real story of Christopher McCandless, who leaves society behind to find himself in the wilderness of Alaska . The film explores themes of freedom, isolation, and detaching from society, making viewers question their own values. This proves how drama can leave a lasting impact by making us think about our own lives.

Why The Others Are Not The Best:

While comedy brings joy and laughter, they often focus on entertainment and don't tend to induce deep thought or emotional reflection. Comedies are great for fun, but they rarely leave a lasting impact for viewers to think about important life issues. Similarly, action movies provide thrilling, high-entertaining experiences with amazing stunts and visual effects,but they often focus more on action and visuals than on meaningful stories. Action movies often lack the emotional depth and complication of human relationships that dramas explore.

In Conclusion

All movies have a time and place regardless of drama. Sometimes you need a laugh, sometimes you need some excitement, and sometimes you need something emotional. But that’s the beauty of the industry, there’s something for everybody in every walk of life and for however they’re feeling. What matters is that we can always find a good movie to watch and enjoy, and we always have a variety of options for what that movie might be
