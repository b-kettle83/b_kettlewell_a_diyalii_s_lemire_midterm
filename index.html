Comedy vs Action vs Drama: A Truly Cinematic Battle

Why Comedy Is The Greatest

When you’re watching a movie, you’re there to relax and have fun for one reason or another. Some people get their enjoyment from being scared, or from intense sequences, but everybody can find enjoyment in laughter and just having a good time. Action and drama are very intense genres of movies, which while they may have a time and place, they aren’t as usable options or as widely available to the viewer as a comedy movie might be, whereas with comedy, everyone can always use a laugh. Also, comedy is something nearly everybody will enjoy with friends or family, while something like action or drama asks for a more particular taste that not everybody will have. Comedy can furthermore help lift spirits and weaken the bad things going on by giving an opportunity to just be in the moment and laugh, and as the saying goes, laughter is the best medicine.
